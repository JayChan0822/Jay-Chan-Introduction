我是陈俊，上海音乐学院电影音乐制作专业研究生，师从秦诗乐副教授。曾获丹尼奖国际电子音乐大赛游戏配乐组银奖；管弦作品《水乡记忆》由苏州交响乐团首演；深度参与《逆水寒》等头部游戏大作及陈致逸音乐会音频制作。致力于融合传统管弦与前沿科技，重塑沉浸式听觉体验。

I am Chen Jun, a graduate student majoring in Film Music Production at the Shanghai Conservatory of Music, studying under Associate Professor Qin Shilue. I won the Silver Prize in the Game Scoring category at the Danny Award International Electronic Music Competition; my orchestral work Memories of the Water Town was premiered by the Suzhou Symphony Orchestra. I have been deeply involved in the audio production for major game titles such as Justice Online and Chen Zhiyi's concerts. I am dedicated to integrating traditional orchestration with cutting-edge technology to reshape immersive auditory experiences.
