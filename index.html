MUSIC
PRODUCER.

我是陈俊，上海音乐学院电影音乐制作硕士在读。擅长国风、流行、管弦等风格的音乐。

曾获丹尼奖国际电子音乐大赛游戏配乐组银奖，国潮音乐提名；深度参与《逆水寒》《燕云十六声》等游戏音乐制作、录音制谱、音乐编辑等工作；

致力于融合传统管弦与前沿音乐美学，重塑沉浸式听觉体验。

Jay Chan is a Master’s candidate in Film Music Production at the Shanghai Conservatory of Music. Excels in composing across various genres, particularly Guofeng, Pop, and Orchestral music.

A decorated composer and producer, he was awarded the Silver Prize in the Game Scoring category at the Denny Awards International Electronic Music Competition and received a nomination for "National Style" music.

His artistic mission lies at the intersection of traditional orchestration and cutting-edge music, dedicated to reshaping the boundaries of immersive auditory experiences.
